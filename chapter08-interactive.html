Core Rule

Carbon forms 4 bonds.

C needs 4 lines attached to it.
Always check your H count!
Representations
  • Structural: Show every atom.
  • Condensed: CH₃CH₂CH₃

1. Longest Chain = Parent

3. Branches alphabetically (ethyl before methyl)

QuizPractice Questions ▾
Q1. Isomerism

Pentane and 2-methylbutane are what type of isomers?

Structural Isomers. Same formula (C₅H₁₂), different connectivity.
Q2. Identification

Which group contains a Nitrogen atom attached to a Carbonyl (C=O)?

Amide. (Not Amine, which has no C=O).
Q3. Prediction

Product of: Ethene + Cl₂?

1,2-dichloroethane. An addition reaction breaks the double bond.
